BUDDY
=================================

![front](docs/buddy_routing.png)

## Summary
* Evaluation platform for Epiphany-V
* 1024 signals accessible through FMC connectors
* 16 separate power domains
* Standard socket
* Mini-ITX form factor (170mm x 170mm)
